Bokeh-Django Examples

In these examples there are two different apps being used. One is a Bokeh app (Sea Surface), and the other is a Panel app (Shapes). The following examples show how these two apps are loaded in different ways.

Directory Apps

The examples in this section are loaded with bokeh_django.directory

  • Sea Surface Temp

Document Apps

The examples in this section are loaded with bokeh_django.document

  • Sea Surface Temp
  • Sea Surface Temp (with custom template rendered by Bokeh)
  • Sea Surface Temp (loaded using direct path to Bokeh app file - this is effectively the same as the Document app above.)
  • Shapes (Panel app)

Autoload Apps

The examples in this section are loaded with bokeh_django.autoload

  • Sea Surface Temp
  • Sea Surface Temp (mapped to custom Bokeh handler URI)
  • Shapes (Panel app)
  • Shapes (with URL variables)
